Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Rotation Agenda ("we," "our," or "the app") handles user data, if any, and your rights regarding this data. Please read this policy carefully.

Data Collection:

Rotation Agenda does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not gather, store, or process any data, including but not limited to names, email addresses, location data, or device information.

Third-Party Services:

Rotation Agenda does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs that would require data sharing.

Data Security:

We prioritize the security and privacy of our users, even though we do not collect data. Our app is designed to ensure that any user information is stored locally on device.

Permissions:

Rotation Agenda requires notification permission solely for the purpose of notifying users about upcoming events and agenda items. This permission is necessary to provide you with event reminders.

Advertising and Analytics:

We do not use any advertising networks or analytics services in Rotation Agenda.

Children's Privacy:

Rotation Agenda is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years of age.

Policy Updates:

Any updates to this privacy policy will be made visible within the app. Please check the policy periodically for changes.

Contact Information:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy or this privacy policy, please contact us via the contact form available on our website
